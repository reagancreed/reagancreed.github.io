I’m Reagan Reed, a plant biotechnology researcher with 8+ years of research experience across molecular biology, seed science, and crop improvement. My current work combines gene editing, stress phenotyping, and quantitative analysis to study seed vigor and germination.

Current Research Focus

Gene editing for seed traits

CRISPR-based workflows in tomato to test seed dormancy, germination, and stress resilience hypotheses.

Seed phenotyping under stress

Greenhouse and controlled-condition experiments evaluating germination behavior under temperature and osmotic stress.

Computational analysis

Reproducible pipelines in Python/R and HPC-enabled RNA-seq analysis to support transparent, data-driven conclusions.
