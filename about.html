About

I am a Plant Biology researcher at UC Davis focused on seed quality, germination, and vigor, with work spanning gene editing, greenhouse and field studies, and reproducible data analysis.

Skills

  • Molecular biology: cloning, genotyping, CRISPR vector design and assembly, primer design, PCR, DNA/RNA extraction and electrophoresis, plant tissue culture, and Agrobacterium-mediated transformation.
  • Bioinformatics and computing: RNA-seq pipeline development, data visualization, high-performance computing workflows, genome/transcriptome data mining, UNIX, Python, R, and JMP.
  • Imaging and analysis: photogrammetry, light microscopy (including fluorescence), confocal microscopy, microtomy, and scanning/transmission electron microscopy.
  • Research tools and prototyping: Fusion 360-based 3D modeling, 3D printing, electronic wiring, and scientific workflow design.

Experience

  • Plant Biology Researcher, UC Davis (2020–2026): leading seed trait and stress-tolerance research, greenhouse trials, pollinations and crosses, laboratory setup/management, and trainee mentorship.
  • Plant Propagation Course Instructor, UC Davis (2024, 2025): taught plant propagation in field and lab settings; developed curriculum, prepared labs, and graded student work.
  • Plant Biology Research Assistant, Skidmore College (2017–2019): prepared research/teaching materials, operated microscopy platforms, and supported student training.
  • Horticulture Laboratory and Field Technician, Cornell University (2019): collected phenotypic data and managed greenhouse/field breeding trials in hemp and shrub willow.
  • Plant Biology Research Intern, University of Galway (2019): investigated adhesion biology in a red alga associated with ocean plastic surfaces.
  • Writing Center Tutor, Skidmore College (2018–2019): coached students on writing structure, argument development, and thesis clarity.
  • Imaging Science Research Intern, RIT (2015): evaluated image-sharpening software performance and presented findings to domain experts.

Education

  • University of California, Davis — M.A./Ph.D. in Plant Biology, Cell and Developmental Biology specialization, Designated Emphasis in Biotechnology (2020–2026).
  • Skidmore College — B.A. in Biology (Molecular Biology, Cell Biology, and Genetics concentration) (2016–2019).
  • Skidmore College — B.A. in Classics (Latin concentration) (2016–2019).
